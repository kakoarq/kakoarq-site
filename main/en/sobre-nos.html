Our Story

Our story begins in 2023 with the partnership of architects and urban planners Vinícius Cataldi and Thais Copolla, cousins and business partners, who have been working in civil construction and architecture since 2016 in renowned architectural firms, dedicating themselves with commitment and dedication to all our projects. Combining their experiences and life journeys to create KaKo together, with one goal: to create and materialize dreams.

"Architecture is a state of mind and not a profession."

Le Corbusier

Our Mission

Our goal at Kako is to create and provide environments that inspire and stimulate the emotions of our clients, bringing along practicality and ergonomics to our daily lives.
